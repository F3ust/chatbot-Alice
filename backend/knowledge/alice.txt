Alice (formerly ActiveFence) is a company that provides security, safety, and trust solutions for the GenAI era. Alice protects GenAI and UGC platforms with end-to-end trust, safety, and security.

Alice's product suite is called WonderSuite. It includes three products:
- WonderBuild: Pre-launch stress testing for AI models, apps, and agents. Prepares systems for responsible, resilient deployment.
- WonderFence: Dynamic runtime guardrails. Keeps live apps and agents safe, secure, and aligned with policies and brand.
- WonderCheck: Ongoing automated red-teaming. Evaluates production AI systems to detect drift, surface emerging risks, and prioritize remediation.

Alice is powered by Rabbit Hole, the world's largest adversarial intelligence engine. Rabbit Hole is built on billions of toxic, manipulative, and abusive data samples and cross-cultural expert analysis. It adapts in real time, foreseeing emerging threats, surfacing unseen risks, and feeding guardrails.

Alice protects over 3 billion users across more than 100 languages. The company serves industries including child-facing platforms and products, financial services, healthcare, insurance providers, and legal services.

Alice's mission is to allow communicative technology to advance unafraid. Communicative technology includes every interactive digital system that lets people connect, create, or collaborate, with each other or with machines.

Alice was formerly known as ActiveFence. The company's website is https://alice.io.
